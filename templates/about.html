About Ennovyx

The all-in-one productivity platform built specifically for students

Our Mission

Ennovyx was created with a simple belief: students shouldn't need 5-6 different apps to manage their academic life. We built a unified platform that brings together tasks, subjects, notes, documents, calendars, expenses, and habit tracking into one intelligent system.

Why Ennovyx?

Traditional productivity tools are built for businesses and professionals. Ennovyx is designed from the ground up for the unique needs of students — from managing multiple subjects to tracking assignments, from budgeting limited funds to building study habits.

One Student. One Account. One Dashboard.

Everything connected in your complete student operating system

The Complete Student OS

We call Ennovyx a "Student Operating System" because everything is interconnected. Your tasks link to subjects. Your subjects organize your documents. Your calendar syncs with deadlines. Your AI assistant controls it all. This integration creates a seamless workflow that helps you focus on learning, not managing apps.
