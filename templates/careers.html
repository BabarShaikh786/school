Marketing Intern
Internship
Remote
3-6 months
Part-time / Full-time

We're looking for a creative and motivated Marketing Intern to join our team and help us spread the word about Ennovyx. You'll work directly with the founding team to develop and execute marketing campaigns, create engaging content, and grow our student community.

What You'll Do:

  • Create engaging social media content (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Develop marketing campaigns targeting students and universities
  • Write blog posts, case studies, and email newsletters
  • Analyze marketing metrics and optimize campaigns
  • Engage with our student community and gather feedback
  • Help with product launches and feature announcements

Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
  • Strong writing and communication skills
  • Familiarity with social media platforms and trends
  • Creative mindset with attention to detail
  • Basic knowledge of design tools (Canva, Figma) is a plus
  • Passion for education technology and helping students

What We Offer:

  • Hands-on experience with a fast-growing ed-tech startup
  • Mentorship from experienced marketers and founders
  • Flexible remote work schedule
  • Stipend based on experience
  • Potential for full-time conversion
  • Portfolio-building opportunities
