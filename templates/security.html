Security at Ennovyx

Your academic data is protected by enterprise-grade security

At Ennovyx, security isn't an afterthought—it's built into every layer of our platform. We understand that your academic work, personal notes, and financial data are sensitive, and we've implemented comprehensive security measures to keep them safe.

End-to-End Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption

Firebase Security

Built on Google's Firebase platform with enterprise-grade infrastructure and security protocols

Secure Authentication

Multi-factor authentication support and secure password hashing with bcrypt

Automatic Backups

Daily automated backups ensure your data is never lost, even in worst-case scenarios

Privacy by Design

Your data is yours alone. We never share, sell, or access your personal content

Regular Security Audits

Continuous monitoring and quarterly security assessments by independent experts

Infrastructure Security

Ennovyx leverages Google Cloud Platform's world-class infrastructure:

  • 99.99% uptime SLA with redundant servers across multiple data centers
  • DDoS protection and advanced threat detection
  • Automated security patches and updates
  • Isolated database environments for each user
  • Real-time intrusion detection systems

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data. Export your entire account at any time in standard formats (JSON, PDF, CSV). Delete your account and all associated data permanently with a single click. No hidden retention policies—when you delete, it's gone.

Authentication & Access

We implement multiple layers of authentication security:

  • Secure password requirements (minimum 8 characters, complexity rules)
  • Optional two-factor authentication (2FA) via email or authenticator apps
  • Session management with automatic timeout after inactivity
  • Instant logout from all devices capability
  • Login activity monitoring and suspicious activity alerts

Data Privacy Compliance

Ennovyx is compliant with major data protection regulations:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to our security team at security@ennovyx.com. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.

Best Practices for Users

Help us keep your account secure:

  • Use a strong, unique password for your Ennovyx account
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out from shared or public devices
  • Keep your contact email up to date for security alerts
  • Review your login activity regularly
