Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Ennovyx, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our student productivity platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an Ennovyx account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Password (encrypted)

Academic Data

To provide our services, we store:

  • Tasks and to-do lists
  • Subject information
  • Notes and documents
  • Calendar events
  • Expense records
  • Habit tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Ennovyx platform
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Send you notifications and email summaries (if enabled)
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Respond to your support requests

Important: We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your academic information is yours and yours alone.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, Google's industry-leading cloud platform. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Automatic backups to prevent data loss

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g., Firebase, email services)
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of email notifications
  • Delete your account at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage

You can control cookies through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

7. Children's Privacy

Ennovyx is designed for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through the platform.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@ennovyx.com
