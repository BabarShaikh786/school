Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Ennovyx uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons:

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing

2. Preference Cookies

These cookies allow the platform to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme settings (light/dark mode)
  • Dashboard layout preferences

3. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the platform:

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on the platform
  • Error messages encountered

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

Note: We do NOT use cookies for advertising or tracking you across other websites. All cookies are used solely to improve your Ennovyx experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We use Firebase by Google for authentication and data storage. Firebase may set its own cookies for security and performance purposes. You can learn more about Firebase's cookie usage in Google's Privacy Policy.

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience on Ennovyx.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

In-App Settings

You can manage certain cookie preferences directly in your Ennovyx account settings under "Privacy & Cookies".

What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable essential cookies, you may not be able to:

  • Stay logged in to your account
  • Save your preferences
  • Access certain features of the platform

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes.
